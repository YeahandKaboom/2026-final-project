### 🎮 **HOW TO USE:**

---

#### 🥁 **MAKING BEATS:**
1. **Click Grid** - Toggle beats on/off
2. **Press SPACE** - Play/pause your beat
3. **Change Tempo** - Press T (60-180 BPM)
4. **Load Presets** - Press 1-4 for instant beats
5. **Clear Pattern** - Press C to start fresh

#### 🎤 **WRITING LYRICS:**
1. **Type Freestyle** - Write while beat plays
2. **Press ENTER** - Add line to song
3. **BACKSPACE** - Delete last word/line
4. **Rhyme Words** - Built-in rhyme suggestions
5. **Flow Practice** - Perfect your timing

#### 🎹 **ADVANCED FEATURES:**
1. **Recording** - Press R to record patterns
2. **Save Songs** - Press S to save your creation
3. **Visual Effects** - Watch the audio visualizer
4. **Screen Shake** - Feel the beat impact
5. **Real-time Synthesis** - All sounds generated live
