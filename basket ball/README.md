## 🎮 HOW TO PLAY:

### 🏀 CONTROLS:
- **WASD/Arrow Keys** - Move left/right
- **SPACE/W/Up** - Jump
- **Hold X** - Charge shot power
- **Release X** - Shoot basketball
- **P** - Pause game
- **ESC** - Return to menu

### 🎯 GAMEPLAY:
1. **Select Your Character** - Choose from 8 unique characters
2. **Choose Opponent** - Pick an NBA legend to battle
3. **Play 1v1** - First to 11 points wins!
4. **Shot Clock** - 24 seconds to shoot or turnover
5. **Score Baskets** - Shoot the orange ball through the hoop!
6. **Win the Game** - Outscore your opponent!

### 🏀 BASKETBALL RULES:
- **Half Court** - Play on one side of the court
- **One Hoop** - Score in the single basket
- **Shot Clock** - 24 seconds to attempt a shot
- **Turnovers** - Switch possession when shot clock expires
- **First to 11** - Win by scoring 11 points first
